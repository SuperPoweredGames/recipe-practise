Fry-up Recipe

Description

Fresh tomatoes, mushrooms and eggs, paired with crispy bacon, flame-grilled sausages, baked beans and toast.

Ingredients

  • Pork Sausages, 2
  • Eggs, 2
  • Bacon, 2
  • Mushrooms, 4
  • Tomatoes, 2
  • Sliced bread, 2
  • Heinz Baked Beans, 300g

Steps

  1. Shallow fry sausages on low heat for 5 minutes
  2. Slice tomatoes and mushrooms in half and add to frying pan for further 5 minutes
  3. Add bacon to frying pan for 10 minutes
  4. Crack eggs into second frying pan and cook to preference
  5. Toast bread under grill or in a toaster, then butter
  6. Simmer baked beans in saucepan on medium heat for 8 minutes, stirring often
  7. Add to plate and serve
