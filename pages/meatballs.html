Meatball Recipe

Description

Savoury balls of beef and spice, paired with sumptuous strands of spaghetti , topped in a rich tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • Beef, 500g (20% fat)
  • Eggs, 4
  • Flour, 500g
  • Olive oil, 2tsp
  • Water, 2tsp
  • Tomatoes, 2
  • Onion, 1
  • Chilli Flakes, 10g

Meatballs

  1. Add 500g beef to a mixing bowl
  2. Crack 2 eggs and add to beef
  3. Dice onion finely and add 100g to beef
  4. Mix by hand
  5. Split mixture into palm-size balls and rest at room temperature

Spaghetti

  1. Add 500g flour to worktop, gather to round shape and indent in middle
  2. Crack 2 eggs and add to flour's bowl
  3. Add 1tsp of Olive oil to mixture
  4. Mix by handle, adding 1tsp of water as needed
  5. Roll mixture flat with rolling pin
  6. Use knife to cut into thin strips
  7. Add to fridge for 30 minutes
  8. Boil in pot of water for 10 minutes

Tomato Sauce

  1. Dice tomatoes into fine mixture
  2. Add 1tsp of Olive oil
  3. Add 10g Chilli Flakes
  4. Blend until paste
  5. Add to saucepan and simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Sear Meatballs until browned
  7. Add meatballs to saucepan to finish for 10 minutes
